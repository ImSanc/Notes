# What is System Design?
- It is a set of requirements needed for desgining a system.
 -> decide architecture 
 -> decide components 
 -> decide  modules

#### Note : How they interact with each other 
These all come together to solve a problem which is product development.


## why is it popular?
- Every single "tech" product is a "system" that has been "designed".
- Company are building product and you need to design it.


## Why understanding system design is important?
- This what people do at their job?
- Everything is practical
- You can see it from Day 1 of joining the company
- Once you grow in your career , 80% of your day is spent in this

Therefore system design is relevant for everyone.

## what will we do when we design a system?
- Breakdown problem statement into solvable sub-problems
- decide on key components and responsibilities
- decide on boundaries of each component
- Touch upon key challenges in scaling it
- make our architecture fault tolerant and available
